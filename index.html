MEXICAN PAYROLL MANAGER

This is an application created to manage weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly payroll for companies that want to register for free with less than 100 employees. They can calculate or make deductions for ISR, IMSS, as well as bonus payments for worked holidays, Sunday premiums, and register absences and deduct the corresponding amount. It is a basic payroll processor; a simulator that could be scaled to develop complete payrolls under Mexican labor laws.
